Community Ingrained Wellness

iLead Youth Intervention Program

iLead is a youth-focused program that offers career and social navigation opportunities for youth from low-income communities in Seattle or troubled youth who often struggle in school. The program connects youth with mentors and academic and career preparation programs that aim to economically uplift our youth.

PACS System Navigation

Systems Navigation was designed to assist African families with children and youth in South Seattle neighborhoods to navigate Seattle Public Schools, the Criminal Justice System, and applying for assistance from DSHS and other social service organizations. Families learn how to navigate standardized testing, communicate effectively with teachers, and understand court systems. PACS System Navigation hopes to empower families to be self-sufficient in applying for social services and solving common language barrier-related issues experienced by immigrant families.
